Spotify Music Analysis

This project was insightful. I explored different audio features like dancebility related to popularity score in music. While my initial expectation was that these characteristics would lead to higher popularity, the findings offered a different perspective. Among the top 100 songs each year, higher danceability and energy were actually associated with lower popularity. This was a surprising discovery, pointing towards other factors such as lyrics, the artist, or cultural relevance as potentially more significant in determining a song's popularity.

Overall, this project was both enlightening and humbling. It served as a reminder that in research, our initial assumptions can sometimes be challenged, leading to unexpected and insightful discoveries.
